About Me

Hi, I’m Felix, a Financial Engineering student at JKUAT with a strong passion for building things on the web. I started learning web development three years ago, and it quickly became more than just a hobby—it's now one of the ways I express creativity and solve real problems. Over the years, I’ve worked on several web projects that have helped me grow my skills, learn new technologies, and understand how good design and clean code come together.

Outside academics and coding, I’m a huge fan of online multiplayer games. I love the teamwork, strategy, and fast thinking they demand. Music is another big part of my life—it helps me focus, relax, and sometimes even inspires my ideas. I also enjoy travelling, exploring new places, and seeing the world from different perspectives.

I’m always learning, experimenting, and pushing myself to become better—both as a developer and as a person. My goal is to combine my financial engineering background with tech to build smart, impactful solutions.

"The web is what you make of it. I strive to create experiences that are not only visually appealing but also intuitive and accessible to everyone."
